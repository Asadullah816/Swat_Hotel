About Swat Hotel

Indulge in Comfort and Elegance with Our Stylish Accommodations

Located amidst the lush green hills and serene environment, Swat Hotel offers a mesmerizing landscape with peaceful feelings to the visitors. Away from the hustle and bustle of the city life, the location of the hotel is home to tranquility making it an ideal residency.

We are proud to offer you services to the best of your comfort and satisfaction. The administration and the staff of the hotel are at your service to cater to anything regarding your comfort.

In addition, the location of the hotel makes it convenient for the guests to easily access the near by popular attractions of the area including Swat Museum, City Centre, Ladies Park, Swat River, White Palace, as well as markets and hospitals.

The staff is available 24 hours at your service.

Laundry Services and ironing facilities are available.

Restaurant has capacity of about 80 guests at a time.

24-hour power supply, with instant backup.

We have an Advance Card scan door lock system.

A nearby walking track is available to warm you up.

Car Parking protected from sun and rain.

Rent-a-car service with variety of vehicles.
